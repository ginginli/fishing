How Skills Work

Titan Fishing has 30+ skills across 5 rarity tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary, and Mythical. Each skill is bound to Z or X and has a cooldown ranging from 5s to 40s.

The key metrics when evaluating a skill are: total damage output, cast duration (how long the animation takes), and whether the cast window allows other skills to recharge — enabling combos.

  • ⚔️ Z slot — primary skill (usually your combo anchor like Reit)
  • 🔥 X slot — secondary skill (burst damage or finisher)
  • ⏱️ Cooldown — shorter CD = more casts per fight
  • 🔄 Combo window — cast time should let other skills recharge
  • 🎁 Some skills (Heaven Lifting Hook) are code-exclusive

Best Skill Combos

Maximize DPS with these proven rotations

01

The Reit Loop

Z (Reit) → X (any skill) → Z (Reit) → X → repeat. Reit's 5s cooldown recharges during any X skill cast. Infinite combo, works on all fish sizes.

02

Reit + Glock

Z (Reit) → X (Glock) → Z (Reit) → X (Glock). Both have short cooldowns and continuous damage. Best general-purpose build for farming.

03

Boss Killer

Z (Reit) → X (Shatter the Azure Heaven or GP Rocken) → Z (Reit) while Mythical recharges. Reit fills the 40s Mythical cooldown gap perfectly.

04

Budget Build

Z (Reit) → X (Gate Clow) → Z (Reit). Fully viable with Rare-tier skills. Gate Clow's cast time lets Reit recharge for a seamless loop.

Skill FAQ

Common questions about Titan Fishing skills

What is the best skill in Titan Fishing?

Reit (Uncommon) is the best skill in the game. Its 5s cooldown makes it the perfect combo anchor — it recharges during any other skill's cast animation, enabling infinite damage loops.

Are Mythical skills worth rolling for?

Yes, but only Shatter the Azure Heaven and GP Rocken are truly top-tier. Unity of Heaven and Man and The Dragon Place have significant drawbacks. Reit (Uncommon) outperforms most Mythicals in practical use.

How do I get Mythical skills?

Mythical skills can be obtained through gacha rolls (Skill Books) or by defeating world bosses. Boss-exclusive Mythicals include Shatter the Azure Heaven, GP Rocken, and Unity of Heaven and Man.

What is Heaven Lifting Hook?

Heaven Lifting Hook is a Legendary skill obtained exclusively through redeem codes. It doubles the damage of your next skill but has a very long cast time and 40s cooldown, making it impractical in most builds.

What does "combo window" mean?

Combo window refers to the cast duration of a skill. If Skill A takes 8 seconds to animate and Skill B has a 5s cooldown, you can use B → A → B in a loop because B recharges during A's animation.
