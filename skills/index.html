How Titan Fishing Skills Work

In Titan Fishing, each skill is bound to Z or X and activates during the fishing battle phase. Titan Fishing skills have a cooldown ranging from 5s to 40s and a cast animation that locks you in place while dealing damage.

The key metrics when evaluating a Titan Fishing skill are: total damage output, cast duration, and whether the cast window allows other skills to recharge — enabling combos.

  • ⚔️ Z slot — primary Titan Fishing skill, usually your combo anchor
  • 🔥 X slot — secondary Titan Fishing skill for burst damage or finishing
  • ⏱️ Cooldown — shorter CD = more casts per fight
  • 🔄 Combo window — cast time should let other skills recharge
  • 🎁 Heaven Lifting Hook is a code-exclusive Titan Fishing skill

Titan Fishing Skill FAQ

Common questions about Titan Fishing skills

What is the best skill in Titan Fishing?

Gate Block (Uncommon) is the best skill in the game. Its 5s cooldown makes it the perfect combo anchor — it recharges during any other skill's cast animation, enabling infinite damage loops.

Are Mythical skills worth rolling for?

Yes, but only Shatter the Azure Heaven and Gate Pole Broken are truly top-tier. Unity of Heaven and Man and The Dragon Place have significant drawbacks. Gate Block (Uncommon) outperforms most Mythicals in practical use.

How do I get Mythical skills?

Mythical skills can be obtained through gacha rolls (Skill Books) or by defeating world bosses. Boss-exclusive Mythicals include Shatter the Azure Heaven, Gate Pole Broken, and Unity of Heaven and Man.

What is Heaven Lifting Hook?

Heaven Lifting Hook is a Legendary skill obtained exclusively through redeem codes. It doubles the damage of your next skill but has a very long cast time and 40s cooldown, making it impractical in most builds.

What does "combo window" mean?

Combo window refers to the cast duration of a skill. If Skill A takes 8 seconds to animate and Skill B has a 5s cooldown, you can use B → A → B in a loop because B recharges during A's animation.
